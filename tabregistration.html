Registration

The cost per delegation is $80 and the cost for each individual delegate is $40. The cost for delegates wishing to register independent of a delegation is $50. Unlike in years past, the last day to change the size of a delegation is October 10th. After that date, we ask schools pay for the number of delegates registered, even if they are unable to fill all of those spots. There is no cost for faculty advisers. Please make checks payable to Horace Mann Model United Nations and mail to:
     Horace Mann School
     Attention: Model United Nations
     231 West 246th Street
     Riverdale, NY 10471
