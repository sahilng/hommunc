Welcome to HoMMUNC XXVIII

Welcome to the website for the Horace Mann Model United Nations Conference. For the past 27 years, we have hosted HoMMUNC to provide an opportunity
for high school students to engage in stimulating debate and hone their speaking, writing, and leadership skills. In 2012, around 900 delegates from 4 different
states attended HoMMUNC XXVI and made HoMMUNC the largest single-day High School Model United Nations Conference in the Northeast.

HoMMUNC XXVIII will be held on October 25th 2014 at the Horace Mann School.
Registration is now open! Please explore this site to learn about HoMMUNC and the HM MUN team. We look forward to seeing you in October! -HM MUN
